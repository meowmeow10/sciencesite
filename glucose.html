How is Glucose made and used in plants?

Glucose is made by photosynthesis. It is made by the leaves, and stored as starch in the roots. Glucose can be turned into many things, including making ammino acids.

Oils and Fats

Glucose can be turned into Lipids. Lipids are fats and oils. Lipids are stored in seeds. Seeds can also store starch.

Protein Synthesis

Glucose can be combined with Nitrates and make ammio acids, and the ammino acids are then turned into proteins. The proteins are then used to make and repair cells.
